Get your free TMDB key

Free Movies and Series uses the free TMDB API to load movie & TV data. It takes about 2 minutes to set up.

  • Create a free account at themoviedb.org/signup
  • Check your email and verify your account
  • Go to Settings → API and request a Developer key
  • Copy your API Key (v3 auth) — it's a long string of letters and numbers

Your key is saved only in your browser's localStorage.
It is never sent anywhere except directly to api.themoviedb.org.
